Our Investment Philosophy

We put all investment opportunities through a strict due diligence process to determine if they meet our five-point investment philosophy.

  • Asset Based

  • Low Stock Market Correlation

  • Experienced Management

  • Short Duration

  • 8-20% Target Returns

Expertly Vetted

Our unwavering philosophy allows us to place great focus on the structure of your investment. If all five points are not met, the investment is not offered on YieldStreet. Plain and simple.

  • Asset-based with validated collateral
    Every borrower must offer tangible collateral to secure their loan in the form of an underlying asset. This collateral could be a real estate property, a legal case, equipment like vehicles or even a purchase order for a business.
  • Low stock market correlation
    All offerings must have low correlation to the broader stock market, meaning that your investments are largely unaffected when the stock market is rising or falling.
  • Managed by experienced asset managers
    YieldStreet thoroughly vets the originator managing the investment to confirm that they have a long-standing history in their industry and domain expertise in the associated asset class. Letting you invest alongside the pros.
  • Short in duration
    YieldStreet targets loan durations typically between 1-3 years with scheduled and event based payments, allowing you to regularly reinvest and take advantage of new opportunities.
  • Target annual returns of 8-20%
    Finally, when all other structural aspects are satisfied, we make sure all offerings have a yield profile that fits within what investors demand. Typically, all YieldStreet offerings range between 8-20% target returns, and our investments have an expected 13.5%2 IRR since inception.
