THE PROJECTYIELD STORY

The Investor’s Problem

Milind Mehere is a successful tech entrepreneur who was seeking investment opportunities outside of the stock market that had real collateral and attractive yields.

He soon realized that asset-based investments in asset classes like litigation and real estate were nearly impossible for an accredited investor like himself to access.

These investments were dominated by institutions with high minimums and long holding periods that shut out everyday investors.

Milind knew many other professionals like him were having the same problem, and he felt passionately that they shouldn’t be excluded because they weren’t multi-millionaires or huge corporations. He knew there had to be a solution.

The Borrower’s Problem

Michael Weisz and Dennis Shields built successful careers in identifying and funding worthy borrowers in the fixed income space.

As a traditional fund, they also had to turn away several smaller investors as they couldn’t operationally support large volumes of investors.

They grew frustrated by these antiquated operations and were determined to solve this problem.

The Solution

Michael, Dennis and Milind worked together to create YieldStreet:
A first of its kind technology platform that connects accredited investors to asset-based investment opportunities across multiple asset classes, and provides deserving borrowers with affordable capital.

Curious About Our Logo?

sym·bi·o·sis

noun

A mutually beneficial relationship between different people or groups.

Our logo represents an impala and oxpecker living in symbiotic harmony. The oxpecker forages on organisms harmful to the impala and alerts the impala to impending danger. In exchange, the impala provides the oxpecker with shelter and food.

At YieldStreet, investors and borrowers also share a beautiful symbiotic relationship. YieldStreet investors access carefully curated asset-based investments provided by our borrowers. In exchange, our borrowers receive the capital they need to achieve their goals.

At YieldStreet we are building a marketplace that enables this ultimate symbiotic relationship between investors and borrowers to flourish.
